PLANNING YOUR STAY

Situated on the picturesque Tay estuary, Dundee is Scotland's sunniest city. Whether you are travelling to the City on business, catching up with friends & relatives or on a family holiday, Dundee has no shortage of options of places to stay for its visitors.

From quaint Bed & Breakfasts to High-end Golf resorts, their truly is the ideal choice for everyone in this incredible City and it's surrounding Areas. Detailed on this page are a selection of some of the best places to stay make your stay in Dundee one that you will be sure to remember.

MALMAISON DUNDEE

Located right in the centre of the city, just across the road from Dundee train station. Take your pick from 91 sumptuous rooms and suites spanning six fabulous floors. The stunning Victorian building offers fabulous facilities and amenities yet retains and respects original features as they were built in 1899.

With a fabulous contemporary restaurant and a stylish cocktail bar, The Malmaison has everything you need in a city centre hotel. Plus, the softest of beds for sleepy heads, and sought after spaces for meetings, parties, events and show-stopping soirées.

HOLIDAY INN EXPRESS

Located in the heart of the city centre, the Holiday Inn Express Dundee is within walking distance of the city's top attractions. Dundee station is a five minute walk away. The Hotel is within easy travelling distance of Edinburgh, Perth, Stirling and Glasgow.

APEX CITY QUAY HOTEL & SPA

Just a short stroll from the city’s most famous attractions and home to one of the country’s best day spas, the stylish Dundee Apex Hotel is the perfect place to rest your head. Take a dip in the pool, unwind in the spa and tuck into delicious Scottish-inspired cuisine before you sink into a dreamy bed for a great night’s sleep.

CARNOUSTIE GOLF HOTEL & SPA

Carnoustie Golf Hotel offers extensive facilities including 95 luxury en suite bedrooms and ten suites with amazing views over the Championship course, the sea and the local town of Carnoustie. Extensive spa and health facilities include a large heated swimming pool, sauna, whirlpool, steam room and for the more energetic a fully equipped gymnasium.

While staying at the Hotel you can follow in the footsteps of some of history’s greatest golfers and test yourself to a challenging round on the notably inspiring Carnoustie Championship golf course. With 12 event rooms accommodating up to 700 delegates this is one of Scotland’s foremost conference venues, ideally located just 10 miles from Dundee with several handy public transport options.

Many bedrooms have views over the 1999, 2007 & 2018 Open Championship golf course or Carnoustie Bay. The newly refurbished Calders Bistro offers delicious fare, much of it from local Scottish produce.

DUPPLIN 152 BED & BREAKFAST

Visit Scotland, quality assured 4 stars. Dupplin152 is based in the west end of Dundee, having been a doctors surgery since 1877. It retains many of its original features and has been owned, renovated and cared for since 1997.
