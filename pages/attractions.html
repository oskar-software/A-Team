Attractions in Dundee

Dundee is a city that has many unique experiences to offer everyone - from outstanding museums like the newly opened V&A Museum, old historical castles, modern golf courses and parks to gyms and swimming pools, escape rooms, cinemas, indoor karts - Dundee has it all! Make sure you visit at least 3 of our top suggestions below:

Cultural attractions

V&A Dundee

The first ever dedicated design museum in Scotland, V&A Dundee is a place of inspiration and learning through design. Visitors describe the building as fabulous with a stunning location and beatiful views. The museum offers exciting workshops and exhibitions open for everyone. Check opening times here.

Broughty Ferry Castle

This fascinating castle built in 1496 on a rocky promontory, has a lot of rich interesting displays and artwork. The old spiral stairs will take you to the top, form where you can enjoy magnificent views of the beach and across the river Tay. Fantastic for children, the castle offers free admission for everyone.

Discovery Point

Situated right next to the V&A Museum, Discovery Point is a top visitor attraction which centres round the Antarctic research vessel RRS Discovery. The ship tells fascinating stories of the most heroic adventures and it is a great day out for all the family. Learn more here.

McManus Galleries

The amazing building of this museum and art gallery, will take your breath away. Knowleageable staff and engaging ehibitions and art compositions, it is definitely worth a visit. Feel free to treat yourself to a nice cup of coffee at the small cafe in the same building. For opening times, click here.

Nature

Camperdown Park

This is one of the loveliest parks around Dundee consisting of 400acres grounds, a famous golf course and a wildlife centre. Camperdown has lots of free fun play areas for kids and free parking to take advantage of while visiting the zoo. For more details click here.

Broughty Ferry Beach

You can walk along this beach and enjoy the sea birds and the stunning views of Tayport. Grab an ice-cream on a sunny day and explore the long stretch of a gorgeous sandy beach. Great for a family day out, make sure you spend some time in beautiful Broughty Ferry too.

Dundee Law

Visitors describe The Law as the best viewpoint topped by an impressive war memorial. It is a historical site which also offers stunning views of the whole city, making this spot a must for visitors. Leave your car at the parking or take a hike, Dundee Law will offer spectacular views!

Clatto Country Park

Peaceful walks around reservior and surrounded by wildlife, you wouldn't know you were so close to the city! Great for a walk, this beautiful location is ideal for a family walk through the forest and a picnic in the summer.

Games & Fun

Escape Rooms

Escape Rooms is an challenging experience - being locked into a room with for 60 minutes, you and your team have to find your way out of it in time by solving puzzles and games. Luckily, there are many escape rooms in Dundee - great for team bonding, pick the one that will bring you most joy.

Olympia

This swimming pool is a brilliant choice for the whole family and a lot of fun! Fantastic facilities and caring staff, lockers and showers available too. Waves, slides and play areas for the kids and swimming lanes for adults, you will not be disappointed. Check fees and opening times here.

Cinemas

Huge screens and cosy seats, Dundee has two cinemas, both located near the city centre with free car park and restaurants nearby. Accesible locations and top notch sound systems, hurry and purchase your tickets at Cineworld or Odeon.

Go Karting

You and your friends and family can enjoy a really exciting race experience at the indoor go-kart circuit in Dundee. Wide track, friendly staff, unforgettable visit and breakdown of your laptimes! Book in advance here.
